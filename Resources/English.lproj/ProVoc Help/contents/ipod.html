Training your vocabulary with your iPod

You can send vocabulary on your iPod (including audio) in the two following formats:

  • Questionnaire: use this format to train your vocabulary on your iPod, using the selected training mode.
  • Glossary: use this format to browse your vocabulary on your iPod. Alphabetical indexes are created both for languages.

To send vocabulary on your iPod:

  • Connect your iPod to the computer.
  • Select Vocabulary > Send to iPod or click on the iPod icon that appears above the training modes.

To access to your vocabulary with your iPod:

  • Select Extras > Notes

To return to the main menu of your iPod (note that this option varies depending on your iPod, see Apple article):

  • With 5th generation iPods (iPod Video and iPod Nano, version 1.1 or later): keep Menu pressed.
  • With other iPods: reset your iPod. Learn how to reset your iPod

To delete vocabulary from your iPod:

  • Select ProVoc > Preferences > iPod or click on the iPod button that appears on the bottom right corner.
  • Select the files that you want to delete and click the Ð button.

If you want to access to other nodes on your iPod:

  • Select ProVoc > Preferences > iPod and check the option Allow other notes to be read with iPod.

Requirement

iPod with Note Reader, i.e. any iPod with dock connector. The iPod must be usable as a disk. If no iPod icon appears on the desktop when the iPod is conntected, open iTunes, select Preferences > iPod > Music and select the "Enable disk use" checkbox.

Note

Due to the limitations of the iPod Note Reader, not all features of ProVoc are available when training vocabulary with your iPod (such as larger text size, repetition of wrong answers, etc.).

Also, if you vocabulary includes audio, you will have to set manually the repeat setting to off (in Settings > Repeat).
