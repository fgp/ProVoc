Training your vocabulary

To train your vocabulary:

  • Select the desired lessons.
  • In the Training section, select the desired training mode.
  • Click or select Vocabulary > Start Training (⌘R).

During the training, you can mark a word (, ⇧⌘F) or ask to display the solution (Give Solution, ⌘G).

When the solution is displayed, you can decide whether the answer you entered was actually correct by clicking Accept Answer (⌘A).

Hold down Option (⌥) to reveal additional options, such as Finish, Edit (to edit the current word), or Give Hint (to display successively the first letters of the solution).
