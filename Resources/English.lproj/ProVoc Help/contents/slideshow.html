Slideshow

By default, a training starts with a slideshow of the new words. To disable this behavior:

  • Click the button or double-click the selected training mode in the list
  • Deselect the checkbox "Start with slideshow of new words"

To start a slideshow of all the words in the selected lessons:

  • Choose Vocabulary > Start Slideshow (⌥⌘R)
