Adding images and movies to your vocabulary

Each pair of word can be complemented with an image and/or a movie.

iSight

To capture a picture or record a video clip with a recording device (e.g. Apple iSight or any FireWire digital video camera) connected to your computer:

  • In the Editing section, display the Inspector (⌘I)
  • Click on the button to take a snapshot
  • Click on the button to record a movie

To add an existing media file (image in any format, or movie compatible with QuickTime) to a word:

  • Drag the file and drop it onto the desired word in the ProVoc list, or
  • Click the button in the Image or Movie section of the Inspector and select Import

The picture icon or video icon in the word list indicate that an image or a movie is assigned to a word pair. To display it in full size, double-clic on the icon.

Image Quiz

To train images (or movies) as a quiz, select the following training mode options: Multiple choice questions and then Multiple choices with images.
