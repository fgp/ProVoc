Managing lessons

Words can be grouped into lessons and chapters, which are displayed in the list on the left of the document window.

  • To create a new lesson, click the + button.
  • To create a new chapter, or to delete an element from the lesson list, click the button.

To move, copy or rearrange words, lessons or chapters within a document or between different documents:

  • Use Edit > Copy and Paste,
  • or select and drag elements to the desired destination. Keep the Option key (⌥) down to copy the elements instead of moving them.
