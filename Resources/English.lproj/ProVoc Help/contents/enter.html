Entering words

To enter your list of words, click on the tab Editing.

  • To enter a new word, enter both languages in the corresponding fields and click Enter.
  • To delete one or more words, select them in the list and press the Backspace key or select Edit > Delete.
  • To modify a word, double-click it in the list. To move to the next column, press Tab. To move to the next row, press Enter or Return.

Comment are typically used to enter context information, or hints about particular forms.

Tip: If you want to enter a list of words column wise (i.e. first by filling all words in one language, then in the other), double-click the last (empty) row of the list and press Enter or Return after each word.
