Managing word lists

Words can be sorted by any of the displayed column.

  • To sort the words by a column, click the column header.
  • To reverse the sort order, click the triangle that appears to the right of the column heading when the column is selected.
  • To return to the original order of words, click the leftmost column heading (above the numbers).

Columns can be freely rearranged.

  • To reorder a column, click the column header and drag it to the desired position.
  • To hide or show columns, choose Edit > View Options, and select the columns you want to appear. (You can also press the Control key and click any column heading to see a list of the view options.)

Contents of columns can easily be modified.

  • To swap the text contents of two columns, choose the corresponding item in the Edit menu.
  • To delete the contents of a column, press the Control key, click any column heading and select Delete Column Contents.
