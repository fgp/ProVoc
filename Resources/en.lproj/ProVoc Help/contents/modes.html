ProVoc Help

Training modes

For optimal learning results, vocabulary can be trained in various ways. With ProVoc, you can create and customize any number of training modes.

  • To create a new training mode, click the + button below the corresponding list.
  • To create, duplicate or remove a training mode, click the button.
  • To modifiy the settings of a training mode, click the button or double-click the training mode in the list. The different parameters of a training mode are explained below.

Multiple choice questions

Check this option to select answers from a list of possible answers. If you don't want the choices to be displayed immediately, check the option Delayed.

Direction

Select with this option the language of the questions and of the answers (version, theme, both or random).

Method

ProVoc offers three different methods to train your vocabulary:

  • Normal: The translation of each word is asked exactly once. After the training is finished, you can choose to repeat again the words that you didn't translate correctly.
  • Until Learned: The translation of each word is asked until it is answered correctly. The first time a word translated incorrectly, it occurs again after 2 questions, then after 4 questions, and so on.
  • Continuous: Each new word to be translated is chosen randomly. Depending on the selected difficulty level (which can be changed during the training), more difficult words are asked more often.
