Customizing the training backgrounds

To customize the animated background displayed during fullscreen training:

  • Choose ProVoc > Preferences > General
  • Check the option Animated background
  • Select the desired background from the menu

ProVoc backgrounds are essentially Quartz compositions (.qtz files). Quartz compositions can be easily found on the internet, such as from www.quartzcompositions.com. To choose a new background:

  • Choose ProVoc > Preferences > General
  • Check the option Animated background
  • Select Choose from the menu and select the desired Quartz composition file.

About Quartz compositions

Quartz compositions can easily be edited without writing a single line of code with Quartz Composer (part of the Developer Tools available from your Tiger Install DVD). Read the documentation from Apple

Create your own animated background
