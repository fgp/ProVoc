Adding audio to your vocabulary

Each word can be complemented with audio recordings for both languages. To record an audio clip for the selected word:

  • In the Editing section, display the Inspector (⌘I)
  • Click on the microphone button next to the desired language
  • To cancel the recording, press Esc
  • To stop the recording, press any other key or click the mouse button

To add an existing sound file to a word:

  • Drag the file and drop it onto the desired word (in the desired language column), or in the inspector, or
  • Click the button in the Audio section of the Inspector and select Import

The audio icon in the word list indicates that a sound is assigned to a word. To play it, double-click on the audio icon.

Training

To listen to a sound during training, click on the corresponding icon. To automatically play sounds during training, check the training mode option Play automatically.
